Chi-squared test:
 - It is used to compare the alphabetical distributions of plaintext or decrypted
    ciphertext, in order to determine whether the decryption was successful or not. (cryptanalysis, see picture)
 - It is also used to compare the distribution of certain propertiesof genes belonging to different categories. (bioinformatics)

T-test:
 - It was originally used as an economical way to monitor the quality of stout (a type of alcohol)
 - Finds the mean(s) in set(s) of data

Correlation-test:
 - Measures a correlation coefficient, in order to determine how porportional two datasets are.
 - Used to see if two columns of data might be related.

ANOVA-test:
 - Instead of analyzing the mean (T-test), it analyses the variation from the mean.
